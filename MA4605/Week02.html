Week 2 - Inference Procedures with R
Inference Procedures
  • This week we will revise many of the Inference Procedures that you studied in Science Maths 3
  • Inference Procedures refers to Hypothesis Tests and Confidence Intervals collectively (also other procedures)
  • We will look at how to implement these procedures with R.
Non-Parametric Procedures
  • All of the statistical procedures so far employ the assumption of normality (i.e. the data is normally distributed)
  • Quite often, this assumption is not justified, and so many statistical tests are not valid
  • We will look at some statistical procedure that do not require the normality assumption. These procedures are called Non-Parametric Statistics.
Correlation
  • In Science Maths 3, you would have been introduced to the concept of "Correlation"
  • We shall revise the Pearson Correlation coefficient
  • We shall look at a formal hypothesis test for the Pearson Correlation Coefficient
  • Non-Parametric Correlation Coefficients will also be presented.
