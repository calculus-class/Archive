Outline of Exam
  • Officially the assessment is to take place on Week 11.
  • There will be two groups, as per previous in-class assessment.
  • The assessment is worth 20% (15% if you taking the optional midterm.
  • This exam is an open book / time hazard assessment.

The Material will include:
  1. One and Two sample t-tests (including test for equality of variance and paired samples)
  2. Correlation and Fitting Regression Models ( including confidence intervals, AIC, R squared)
  3. Testing Normality (qqplots and shapiro-wilk test)
